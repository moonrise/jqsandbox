Sliding Panel

Sliding left/right panel looks cool, but the layout requires that it occupies entire height of the page. This constraint makes the panel not useful for us because the hierarchy context of the panel appears to be global to all pages. If the panel slides in and out within the content area of the page, it could be more useful.

Additionally the panel content scrolls up and down along with the page content area while the header and footer stays fixed, which looks funny.

What will be good for this panel? Help content? General Settings? Broadcast, notification messsages, etc...?
